Men's Fellowship

On a regular basis the Men of the church gather together for special times of fellowship and Bible study. These events are announced as they develop. They particularly like bowling and breakfast.

Women's Fellowship

Our women meet for fellowship and Saturday morning Bible study. Events are announced as they are planned.

Children's Ministry

Children's Church meet on Sunday mornings, 4 yrs. through 6th grade. Object lessons, crafts, games and Bible lessons make an inspiring atmosphere. These classes begin after our worship ends in the main sanctuary. Children are dismissed to go after our worships ends.
